Movement Coach

Personal training, client tracking, and coach communication in one workspace.

Movement Coach helps trainers and coaches prescribe sessions, review client pillar scores, manage nutrition check-ins, curate education, and keep client communication focused.

Support Privacy Policy

For Coaches

Review client readiness, adjust training, manage nutrition feedback, and replace default educational content with your own YouTube videos or website resources.

For Clients

View assigned workouts, track training and recovery pillars, browse coach-curated education, use timers, and message your coach from a streamlined mobile experience.

Built For Coaching

Movement Coach is not a medical diagnosis tool. It is designed to support coaching workflows, education, accountability, and structured personal training.
