Movement Coach

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

Overview

Movement Coach is a personal training and coaching app. The app is designed to help coaches manage training, client check-ins, nutrition review, education resources, and coach-client communication.

Information We Process

Depending on how the app is used, Movement Coach may process account details such as email address, profile information, coach workspace membership, training assignments, client readiness scores, nutrition review notes, messages, and coach-curated content links.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to provide the coaching workspace, synchronize coach-managed content, support client review workflows, and maintain account access. Movement Coach does not sell personal information.

Service Providers

The app uses Supabase for authentication, database, and storage services. Coach-curated content may link to external websites or video platforms such as YouTube, which are governed by their own privacy policies.

Health And Training Notice

Movement Coach is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnosis. Training, nutrition, and recovery information should be reviewed with an appropriate professional when needed.
